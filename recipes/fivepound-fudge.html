Five Pound Fudge

Description

Do you enjoy milk chocolate that will melt in your mouth? Don't you hate it when your fudge gets rock hard? Well, avoid chipping a tooth, with this divine fudge recipe. Come along on this journey to strengthen your muscles (more on this later) and satisy your cravings while we make Five Pound Fudge!

Ingredients

  • 4 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1 can evaporated milk
  • 1 1/2 sticks of Oleo
  • 2 packages of semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1 1/3 pints of marshmallow cream
  • nuts if desired

Directions

  1. Open up your two pints of marshmallow cream. If you would like to measure it out into a bowl beforehand, that would make it easieer for you to add it in to the pot.
  2. Put sugar, milk and Oleo in heavy large pan
  3. Bring to a boil, cook 8 minutes. Make sure to stirr continuously. rRemove from heat if needed.
  4. Are you ready to build those muscles? Add the marshmallow cream and chips. Stir both in until all are melted together.
  5. Add nuts now if you wish.
  6. Pour into a 9x13 dish
  7. Let cool and cut.
  8. Enjoy!
