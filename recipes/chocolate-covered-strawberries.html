Chocolate Covered Strawberries

Description

Are you looking for a romantic dessert to share with your loved one? Have you suddenly come into a windfall of strawberries and choclate and you don't know what to do with it? Well look no futher, for I have a recipe that suits you perfectly!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of chocolate chips(semi-sweet)
  • 1 1/2 of shortening (Crisco in can)
  • Strawberries with stems

Directions

  1. Rinse the strawberries, dry and chill
  2. Melt the chocolate with the shortening in a large microwaveable container
  3. Stir until smooth
  4. Take your chilled strawberries, hold them by the leaf at an 45 degree angle and dip them 2/3 of the way into the chocolate mixture*
  5. Shake the strawberry gently to remove access chocolate
  6. Place the dipped strawberries on a wax covered plate, allow them to cool or refridgerate
  7. Enjoy!

*Mixture makes about 8-10 strawberries
